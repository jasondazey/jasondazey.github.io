a designer, photographer, traveller

Chicago-based web designer who enjoys creating delightful user experiences.

Travelling has become a more recent passion of mine. I've been to Spain, Portugal, Findland, England, Mexico, Canada, and Ireland. The photo above is the amazingly beautiful Cliffs of Moher from my trip to Ireland during the Fall of 2017. I love to take photos during my adventures! See a small sampling on my Instagram.

I have three dogs. Yes, three – crazy – I know. All three were rescued through a local canine rescue where my wife and I volunteered. Animal welfare – especially canine – is another passion of mine.

Bailey was our first – he is a Beagle/American Staffordshire Terrier. Ollie was our second – he is mainly a Shih-tzu/Maltese with a bit of other small dogs mixed in. And Sam Adams is our third – he is a Boston Terrier.
